BitePins Support Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Photos and location

BitePins asks for Photos permission to scan local photo metadata and classify images on device with Apple's Vision framework. It may use photo location metadata to group food and drink photos into map pins.

BitePins asks for Location permission to show your current location on the map and to scope place search.

Local app data

The first current-team BitePins release stores place pins, categories, photo identifiers, and related metadata in local CoreData storage. CloudKit sync is disabled in this build.

Third-party SDKs

The current binary includes Kakao Local Search, Firebase, Google Mobile Ads, and StoreKit SDKs. App Store privacy labels should match the final SDK decisions before public App Store submission.
