Support

BitePins is an iPhone and iPad app for turning food and drink photos into a personal map.

Basic use

  • Grant Photos permission so BitePins can scan your local photo library.
  • Grant Location permission if you want the map to show your current location.
  • Review photo groups on the map, edit pins, and organize categories.
  • Use place search to attach a pin to a nearby restaurant, cafe, or spot.
