PlainClips Support Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

No data collection

PlainClips does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app has no account system, analytics, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or cloud sync.

Local clipboard history

PlainClips stores plain text clipboard history locally on your Mac. It does not store images, files, or rich text. History is bounded to recent clips and can be cleared from the app menu.

Source app metadata

PlainClips may store the source app name and copied time locally so you can recognize a clip in the history board. This display can be disabled in Preferences.

Accessibility permission

PlainClips requests Accessibility permission only to paste the selected clip into the frontmost app. If permission is denied, the selected clip is still copied to the pasteboard for manual paste.
