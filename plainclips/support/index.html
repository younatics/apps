Support

PlainClips is a menu bar app for plain text clipboard history on Mac.

Basic use

  • Copy plain text in any app.
  • Press Shift-Command-V to open the history board.
  • Use the arrow keys or on-screen chevrons to choose a clip.
  • Press Return to paste, or paste manually if Accessibility permission is not enabled.

Privacy and storage

Clipboard history stays on your Mac. PlainClips does not transmit clipboard content, use analytics, show ads, or require an account.
