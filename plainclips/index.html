PlainClips

A small Mac clipboard history app that keeps only plain text, locally on your Mac.

Built for quick recall

Copy text, press Shift-Command-V, move through recent clips, and paste the one you need. PlainClips skips images, files, rich text, cloud sync, accounts, ads, and analytics.
