Daynest

A shared memory timeline for the moments you make together.

Simple by design

Daynest is not a planner, task list, chat app, or social network. It keeps one quiet timeline of memories with dates, notes, places, emoji, and selected photos.

Private first

Memories are saved locally first and sync with iCloud when available. Sharing uses Apple's native iOS collaboration share sheet. Daynest has no custom accounts, ads, analytics, or tracking.
