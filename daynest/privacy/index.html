Privacy Policy

Daynest is designed to keep shared memories private.

Data collection

Daynest does not collect personal data on a custom server. It does not use analytics, ads, tracking, or third-party SDKs.

Local and iCloud data

Memories are stored on your device. If iCloud is enabled, Apple CloudKit may sync your Daynest data across your devices and with people you invite through the native sharing sheet.

Photos and location

Daynest uses selected photos only when you add them to a memory. Location text is optional. Current-location access is only used if a future Daynest feature asks for it and you grant permission.
