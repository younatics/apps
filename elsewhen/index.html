Elsewhen

A tiny, focused menu bar world clock. See two time zones without clicking — then open it to drag time across the world.

SF 9:30a · NY 12:30p

What it does

  • Shows your chosen cities right in the menu bar, always live.
  • Click to open an Overlap-style scrubber — drag time and every zone moves together.
  • Day/night and working-hours shading make overlapping hours obvious.
  • Add any time zone in the world; rename or pin the ones you care about.
  • 12- or 24-hour time.

Private by design

No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no network. Elsewhen needs no permissions and stores only your city list on your Mac. See the privacy policy.
