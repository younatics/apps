Elsewhen Support

Where is the app after I launch it?

Elsewhen lives in the menu bar, not the Dock. After launch, look at the top-right of your screen for the time string (for example “SF 9:30a · NY 12:30p”). Click it to open the app.

How do I add a city?

Click the menu bar item, then the + button, and search for any city or time zone.

How do I change what shows in the menu bar?

In the popover, use the menu-bar toggle on each city’s row. Double-click a name to rename it; use the × button to remove it.

How does the timeline work?

Drag any track left or right. Every city moves in lockstep so you can see what time it is everywhere at once. Press Now to return to the live current time.
